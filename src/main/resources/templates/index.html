Our Services

Our program is designed to offer free federal income tax filing assistance to clients who earn less than $54,000 USD or less per year. Individuals who may have disabilities and/or are limited English speaking persons may also apply.

Our staff is IRS-certified, and our service is free for basic income tax return preparation with electronic filing for qualified individuals.

Utilize our mobile APP (iOS or Android) to file directly from your smartphone, or complete the contact form below to start the conversation with one of our trained professionals.

How It Works

Submit the needed information and a Team member will be assigned your file and contact you for a phone interview. We will then review Your submitted data and ensure the tax information is correct. Once you approve the return, your return will be submitted for signature (via e-signature either through a mobile device or computer). After the proper signatures have been obtained, the tax return will be submitted to the IRS and your federal tax refund will be deposited on a CashPass Debit Card and mailed to you with 3 – 7 days to the mailing address on your tax return.
That’s it! No charge! No fee! Your tax return has been FREEFILE’d.
