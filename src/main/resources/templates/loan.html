Program Facts

* The Fast Cash ADVANCE has multiple possible loan amounts ranging from $500 to $6,000, giving your taxpayer the best chance for loan approval.

* The Fast Cash ADVANCE is affordable to the taxpayer. The taxpayer may elect to limit its application to an amount that has no interest or other fees. If the taxpayer elects to apply for the entire Fast Cash ADVANCE available to them, they are charged an interest rate of approximately 36% on the total loan amount.

* The Fast Cash ADVANCE product cost is paid by any interest paid by the taxpayer and fees you, as the preparer, pay. TPG has created an affordable program with reasonable product fees depending on the loan amount the taxpayer is approved for

* Customers can elect to receive the Fast Cash ADVANCE via cashier’s check or Green Dot Walmart Money card.

* Customers can visit here to monitor their account status and get updates on their Fast Cash ADVANCE.

* Once the IRS issues the taxpayer’s refund, TPG will deduct the Fast Cash ADVANCE amount from the refund amount.
